# Fight Cam Poster Images

Place your professional poster images in this directory with the following naming convention:

- `poster1.png` - Poster for live video 1
- `poster2.png` - Poster for live video 2
- `poster3.png` - Poster for live video 3
- `poster4.png` - Poster for live video 4
- `poster5.png` - Poster for live video 5
- `poster6.png` - Poster for live video 6
- `poster7.png` - Poster for trending video 1
- `poster8.png` - Poster for trending video 2
- `poster9.png` - Poster for trending video 3
- `poster10.png` - Poster for trending video 4
- `poster11.png` - Poster for trending video 5
- `poster12.png` - Poster for trending video 6

## Specifications

- **Format**: PNG (recommended for quality and transparency support)
- **Resolution**: 640x360px (16:9 aspect ratio)
- **File Size**: Optimized for web (< 500KB per image recommended)
- **Content**: Professional fight/sports themed posters with clear imagery

## How Posters Work

- Posters display as an overlay on video cards
- When a video starts playing or is clicked, the poster fades out
- The poster image serves as a thumbnail before the video loads
- Clicking on the poster plays the video

The component automatically cycles through all 12 posters for different video categories.
